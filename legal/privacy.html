Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-10-18

StrataVariance Labs (“SVL”, “we”) operates public validation sites and related tooling (including Ripple, Keel, etc.). This page describes what we collect and why.

What we collect

  • Basic site logs (timestamps, IPs as part of standard HTTP logs) for reliability and abuse prevention.
  • Metrics you explicitly publish to public logs (e.g., transparency JSONL). These are public by design.
  • Contact information you send us directly (e.g., email) for support.

How we use data

  • Operate, secure, and improve our sites and validation frameworks.
  • Publish public transparency artifacts you opt into.

Data sharing

We do not sell personal data. We may use standard third-party hosting/CDN and CI/CD providers to run the sites.

Retention

Operational logs are kept as short as practical for reliability and security. Public artifacts remain public.

Your choices

Contact us to request removal of non-essential data you provided directly where feasible.
